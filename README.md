## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Are all these PDF tools really free?
Yes, every tool in this directory is completely free. There are no paid tiers, no feature gating, and no watermarks added to your output files. We sustain our platform through unobtrusive display ads, but never force them between your workflow.

### Do I need to create an account to use these PDF tools?
Absolutely not. **No signup, no email, no registration** is one of our core design principles. You can use every tool as a guest, infinitely.

### Will my PDF file be uploaded to your server?
No. All PDF editing, conversion, merging, and signing occur **locally in your browser** via client‑side technology. Your file stays on your device at all times.

### Can I use these tools on my mobile phone?
Yes, every tool is fully **mobile responsive**. You can compress, sign, or convert PDFs on iOS and Android browsers like Safari and Chrome.

### What is the largest PDF file I can process?
There is no artificial file size limit. However, since processing happens on your device, very large files (hundreds of MB) may be limited by your device’s memory. For best performance, we recommend splitting huge PDFs first if they become sluggish.

### Do you store my signed document or signature?
No. The signature drawing or image you upload for signing is processed locally and discarded when you close the tab. No signature database exists.

### Can your tools handle scanned PDFs with OCR?
Yes. Use the **OCR PDF** tool to convert scanned, image‑based PDFs into searchable text. It works offline‑first, meaning the OCR engine runs in your browser.

### How is Toolimi different from other free PDF websites?
Most “free PDF” sites upload your file to a server, process it there, and often store it temporarily. Toolimi does **zero‑server processing**. Additionally, we never force a signup or add watermarks. The combination of true privacy, instant access, and full feature depth sets us apart.

### Do you offer batch processing?
Some tools like Merge PDF can handle multiple files at once. For batch conversion (e.g., many PDFs to JPG), you currently need to process one at a time. We are exploring batch capabilities for future releases.

### How often do you add new PDF tools?
We regularly expand our toolset based on user feedback. Our goal is to maintain the most comprehensive **free online PDF toolkit** possible. If you have a suggestion, open an issue in this repository.
